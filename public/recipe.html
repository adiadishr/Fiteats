Seafood Platter

@spongebob

4,000 views

12/12/2024

Description:

4.0

Rating

245

Calories

Delightful flavors of the ocean - bursting with a variety of fresh seafood and vibrant ingredients, this dish offers a perfect balance of taste and nutrition. Whether you're looking for a wholesome meal or hosting a gathering, this recipe promises to satisfy your cravings while keeping you on track with your health goals.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh shrimp
  • Succulent Scallops
  • Fragrant garlic cloves
  • Zesty Lemon Wedges
  • High-quality olive oil

Instructions:

  • Prep the Seafood: Begin by rinsing the seafood under cold water and patting dry
  • Sauté the Vegetables: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  • Cook the Seafood: In the same skillet, add the seasoned seafood in a single layer. Cook for a few minutes on each side
  • Garnish with Herbs: Sprinkle freshly chopped herbs like thyme, rosemary, and parsley
  • Serve and Enjoy: Arrange the seafood and vegetables on a platter
